Our Story

Home is where everything begins. Here at Bengkel Kayoe, we make sure to celebrate and treasure every part of the home that tells who you are. With more than 50 years of experience, we are proud to present a wide selection of quality furniture to meet your home and business needs. We only use the best wood materials such as teak, mahogany and other solid woods which are carefully selected, resulting in products that are durable and have high aesthetic value.

Apart from that, Bengkel Kayoe offers affordable prices without compromising on quality, because we believe that premium furniture should be enjoyed by everyone. From tables, chairs, cupboards, to home decor, our collections are designed to create a comfortable, functional and stylish atmosphere. Make Bengkel Kayoe part of your story, because each of our products is designed to bring warmth and comfort to every corner of your room. Find your dream furniture only at Bengkel Kayoe, because your trust has been our passion since 1974.
